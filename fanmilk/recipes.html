Tropical Sunset
Ingredients:

FanDango Citrus Drink.
FanDango Tropical Drink.
Blackcurrant Cordial (or Grenadine).
Ice.
Lime wheel (Garnish).

How To:

Add Ice to a long glass.
Half fill the glass with equal measures of FanDango Citrus and Tropical Drink into the glass.
Tilt the glass and add 1 measure of Blackcurrant Cordial (or Grenadine) to the glass
Garnish with Lime wheel.
Enjoy.

Banana Longboat
Ingredients:

1 Banana
FanIce Triple Treat Ice Cream
Chocolate, Strawberry and Pineapple Syrups
Whipped Cream
Cherry (to garnish)

How To:

Split a banana half in a sundae bowl.
Serve – on the banana, 1 scoop each of Chocolate, Vanilla and Strawberry Ice Cream – from your tub of FanIce Triple Treat.
Garnish the Ice Cream scoops with the syrups of your choice -Chocolate, Pineapple and Strawberry syrups.
Spray some Whipped Cream onto the Ice Cream scoops.
Cherry (to garnish)

Fruity Smoothies
Ingredients:

Fantastic Yoghurt Drink
1 cup, chopped fruits. Watermelon, Mangoes, Pawpaw, Bananas, Strawberries – or your preferred fruit
½ cup of crushed Ice

How To:

Pour in ½ cup or crushed Ice into a blender.
Add 4 measures of Fantastic Yoghurt Drink.
Add 1 cup of your chopped fruits.
Blend all ingredients in the blender for 10 – 15 seconds
Pour content in a long glass and put in a jumbo straw.
Enjoy.
